The Problem

India's MSMEs pay a
Fragmentation Tax

Unlike China's clustered factory zones, India's 63 million small businesses are scattered — paying premium prices for being small and alone.

01
Geographical Fragmentation
Indian MSMEs are geographically isolated, unable to leverage the collective bargaining power that massive Chinese factory clusters enjoy by default. A furniture maker in Jalandhar has no connection to the lumber buyer two streets away.
02
Cost Inequality
Isolated businesses pay premium prices for raw materials. Small order quantities mean no volume discounts — and high per-unit logistics costs that eat into already thin margins.
03
The Scale Gap
Being small is expensive. Without "strength in numbers," MSMEs cannot compete on price against large players or global factories — trapping them in a low-margin, high-cost cycle.

Key Features

Built to be trusted at scale

Every feature is designed to remove friction, build trust, and ensure deals actually go through.

Core Differentiator
🛡️
The Trust Protocol (Verified Onboarding)
Every participant is verified using official business registration data (MSME Udyam, GST, PAN). No fake accounts, no ghost orders. Only real, trusted businesses join a cluster — making every commitment legally grounded and commercially serious.
📍
Proximity-Based Matching Engine
Our geospatial algorithm automatically surfaces partners within a 50km radius — keeping intra-cluster shipping costs minimal while maximising order size for bulk discounts.
📊
The Deal-Meter Dashboard
A real-time live dashboard shows how close your group is to unlocking the next price tier. Visual, motivating, and transparently honest about what's needed to hit the discount threshold.
🔒
Secure Order Commitments
Smart digital commitments lock in each participant's order before the deal executes. Zero ghosting. Zero last-minute dropouts. The system holds every member accountable so deals always close.
🚛
Milk Run Logistics
One optimised truck route delivers to all four or five cluster members in a single trip — dramatically cutting per-unit shipping versus individual deliveries. Think shared Uber, but for factory freight.

How It Works

From post to delivery
in 4 steps

1
Step 1 — The Post
Manufacturer lists a material need
A workshop owner posts their requirement — e.g., "Need 500kg Aluminum, Grade 6061" — in two clicks. The platform logs quantity, grade, timeline, and preferred location for delivery.
📋
2
Step 2 — The Match
App instantly finds nearby neighbours with same need
The geospatial matchmaking engine scans verified businesses within 50km and surfaces three other manufacturers who need the same material — right now. No manual search needed.
🔍
3
Step 3 — The Pool
Group hits the bulk discount target → order locked
As partners confirm their quantities, the Deal-Meter fills up in real time. Once the threshold is hit, the combined order is locked in — locking in the bulk price for every participant simultaneously.
⚡
4
Step 4 — The Milk Run
One truck delivers to all four shops in one trip
A single freight vehicle follows an optimised multi-stop route, delivering to all cluster members. Shipping cost per business drops by up to 50% versus placing individual orders separately.
🚚
